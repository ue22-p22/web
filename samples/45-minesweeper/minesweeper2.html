how to play

  • use click on a hidden cell to see how many of its neighbors are bombs
  • use option-click or meta-click on a hidden cell to mark it as a bomb; a second time will undo that
  • use option-click or meta-click on an unveiled cell: if you have found the right number of bombs in its immediate vicinity, it will clear all the other neighbors
  • if an unveiled cell has been marked as bomb, clicking it will remove the bomb mark
